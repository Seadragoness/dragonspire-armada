1. To leave the pack, you must challenge or seek permission.

To preserve secrecy, wolves may only leave if they defeat the Warlord or a chosen proxy. In rare cases, trusted members may be granted permission.

2. No harm is to come to our allies, unless in self-defense.

No offensive action may be taken against allies unless in self-defense. If attacked, leadership must be informed for potential retribution.

3. Trespassers and visitors must be met by leadership.

Intruders beyond public areas must be intercepted by Tier 2+ wolves. Lower ranks may intercept in emergencies but must call for higher ranks immediately.

4. All members are entitled to basic necessities.

Medicine, food, bedding, and essentials must be available to all, including servants.

5. Treat fellow members with respect.

No wolf should suffer abuse from another, aside from injuries sustained in training or punishment.

6. The alpha's word is law.

The alpha's command is absolute. Severe punishments may apply for disobedience, especially during wartime.

7. Show restraint, respect, and dignity.

All members must conduct themselves honorably and not tarnish the pack's reputation.

8. Mandatory meetings must be attended.

Intentionally missing a meeting is a serious offense and may result in rank loss or punishment.

9. Every member must contribute.

All wolves must contribute to the pack's well-being. Wolves found lazy or unworthy will be demoted to servants or slaves.

10. Reproduction is not a right./Armada is currently closed for litters

All wolves must request permission before reproducing. Unauthorized litters will be punished severely. Servants and slaves may not reproduce at all.

11. No wolf may take a mate outside the pack.

To ensure loyalty, wolves may not take mates outside the pack. Casual flings with loners or non-allied wolves are strongly discouraged.

12. The marketplace is to remain neutral.

The marketplace must remain open to all. While enemy packs may be observed, only Tier 2+ ranks can deny service.

13. Activity of Tier 1-2 wolves

Ideally, tier 1-2 wolves should be posted 2-3 times per month to maintain consistency without overwhelming a single person. However, I would like to see some months with at least 5-6 posts periodically to demonstrate sustained effort and engagement.
